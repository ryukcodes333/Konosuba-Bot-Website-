Command Guide
.catchThrow a Pokéball at the wild Pokémon that appears in your group.
.partyView your current battle party of up to 6 Pokémon.
.battle @userChallenge another player to a Pokémon battle.
.dex [name]Look up Pokédex info for any Pokémon species.
.release [id]Release a Pokémon from your collection.
.pokebagCheck how many Pokéballs, Great Balls, and Ultra Balls you have.
.buddy [name]Set a Pokémon as your buddy for bonus XP in battles.
